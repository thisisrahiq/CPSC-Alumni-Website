About CPSC Alumni Association

Our Journey

The CPSC Alumni Association was founded in 2018 with a vision to connect and unite all alumni of CDA Public School & College. What started as a small initiative has grown into a thriving community that bridges generations of students who have walked through the halls of our beloved institution.

Our association has been instrumental in fostering lasting relationships among alumni, creating opportunities for professional networking, and giving back to our alma mater. In 2020, we proudly organized our 1st Alumni Reunion, bringing together graduates from different batches to celebrate our shared memories and achievements.

Today, we continue to grow stronger, with active participation from alumni across various professions and walks of life, all united by the common thread of our CPSC heritage.
